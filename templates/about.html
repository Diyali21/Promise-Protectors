Our Story

In 2025, Anita Chivizhe and Diyali Devraj, friends, partnered up to secure couple’s “Happily Ever Afters” on their wedding day. Having witnessed many challenging moments on couples’ wedding days, Diyali and Anita co-founded “Promise Protectors”

About Promise Protectors

Our mission, to safeguard the joy and dreams of every couple on their special day. We understand that weddings are not just events; they are cherished moments filled with love, hope, and commitment. Our dedicated team is committed to providing tailor-made cover specific to your wedding dream.
